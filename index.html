Hello.

My name is David and I am an entrepreneur from Austin, Texas. My primary focus is photography and I am a Software Developer. I became a professional photographer after spending years as a peer counselor for justice-involved veterans in the Austin area. As an army veteran myself, my love for photography started while I was deployed in Afghanistan. The landscape and people around me were unlike anything I’d seen or experienced growing up in the Texas Hill Country.

My Skills.

Capturing Light and Memories

Since then, photography has been my creative outlet and personal therapy, capturing the world through my own lens. When I don’t have a camera in-hand, I’m out riding my motorcycle, re-reading a Vonnegut book, enjoying a bottle of wine (with others), or travelling….. in which my camera probably is in-hand! I love capturing the authenticity of the moment, rather than a staged photo. I’m drawn to meeting you where you are, so I can capture a memory of you in your natural environment. Telling a story through emotions is the cornerstone of my photography.
